About Me

Once upon a time, a perfectly sane human (yepp, that’s me —> FATIMAH SALIM SIDDIQUI ) decided to make the internet a happier place. MOODIFYY was born from a noble idea: sprinkle joy into people’s lives.

Then reality hit — I spent hours arguing with HTML like it was a stubborn toddler, CSS refused to cooperate like a diva actor, and JavaScript… well, JavaScript just sat there throwing errors like confetti 🎉. There were late-night caffeine sessions, dramatic sighs, and at least three moments where I considered yeeting my laptop out the window.

But here it is — MOODIFYY, alive and running, so please enjoy it… or if you don’t, please send your complaints to my non-existent customer care department 🙃.
